Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Hair Color Changer ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

Photos

When you use the app to change your hair color, your photo is sent to our server for processing. The photo is used solely to generate the hair color result and is not stored permanently on our servers. Photos are automatically deleted after processing is complete.

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google > Ads.

Purchase Data

If you purchase the Premium version, the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Photos: Only to generate hair color change results
  • Advertising data: To display ads (free version only)
  • Purchase status: To determine whether to show ads

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with:

  • Google AdMob – for serving advertisements (free version)
  • Google Play Billing – for processing purchases

4. Data Retention

Photos are processed in real-time and not stored after the result is delivered. No personal data is retained on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device settings
  • Delete the app and all local data at any time
  • Purchase Premium to remove all advertising

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/taaderbe/Hairstyle/issues.
